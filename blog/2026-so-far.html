Science Fair Research

Alongside the musical, I'm also preparing my project for the science fair. My research focuses on how different light wavelengths — specifically red and blue light — affect lipid accumulation in marimo algae.

This is an important topic because lipids store more energy than carbohydrates, which makes them useful for producing biofuels. Algae are especially interesting for biofuel research because they grow quickly and can be cultivated without competing for land used in agriculture.

My Experiment

In my experiment, I'm growing marimo algae under different lighting conditions:

  • Red light
  • Blue light

By keeping other factors like temperature, nutrients, and growth time constant, I can observe how the different wavelengths affect lipid accumulation.

If certain wavelengths encourage greater lipid production, this could help researchers improve methods for producing algae-based biofuels.
