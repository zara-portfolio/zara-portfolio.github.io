Why I'm Interested in Formula 1

Ever since I watched my first race, the sound of the engines and the screech of the tires have fascinated me. From engineering to endurance, Formula 1 has it all. My favorite teams are McLaren and Ferrari, due to McLaren's speed and style, and Ferrari because of its rich history.

I'm particularly focused on learning more about aerodynamics and pitstops in Formula 1, since pitstop strategy and aerodynamics play a huge part in Formula 1. Recently, I attended the F1 Azerbaijan Grand Prix held in Baku. To say it was exhilarating or a dream come true wouldn't do it justice.

The Azerbaijani Grand Prix was full of dramatic crashes, tight turns, wild weather, and well-deserved podiums. Expect the unexpected indeed!
