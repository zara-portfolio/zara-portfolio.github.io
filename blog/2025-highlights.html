🎄 2025 Year in Review 🎄

2025 has been one of the most memorable and transformative years of my life. I took my acting skills to the next level, exploring both classic monologues like Joan of Arc and contemporary teen pieces through improv and performance work. Each piece helped me grow not just technically, but emotionally — finding new depth and confidence on stage.

I’m especially proud of my role as NHS President at school, where I helped lead our community in meaningful impact. Together, we raised ₸2,009,500 (about $3,900–$4,000 USD) to support a local hospital that provides recovery therapy for disabled children. We also organized food drives that collected over 100 kg of food for a nearby shelter. Seeing how much we could accomplish when we came together was one of the greatest highlights of my year.

On the academic side, I leveled up my coding skills with CS50x, building solid foundations in computer science and problem-solving. Right now, I’m gearing up for the Unified Council Olympiad in January — excited for the challenge and ready to put in the work.

And of course, 2025 was fantastic for me as an F1 fan! Watching Lando Norris secure his maiden championship win was a thrilling moment — one that capped off an unforgettable season.

✨ All in all, 2025 has been a year of growth, service, passion, and achievement — and I’m looking forward to what’s next! ✨
